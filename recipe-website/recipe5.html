Virgin Mojito

Virgin Mojito is a refreshing non-alcoholic drink made with mint, lime, sugar, and soda.


The trick is to gently crush mint leaves to release flavor without bitterness.


Perfect summer drink to stay cool and refreshed.

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Servings: 2

Category: Beverage

Ingredients

  • Mint leaves
  • Lime
  • Sugar
  • Soda

Instructions

  1. Wash the fresh mint leaves thoroughly and place them in a glass.
  2. Add 2 to 3 lime wedges to the glass.
  3. Add 2 teaspoons of sugar (adjust to taste).
  4. Using a muddler or spoon, gently crush the mint leaves and lime together to release the flavors (do not over-crush to avoid bitterness).
  5. Add a handful of ice cubes to the glass.
  6. Pour chilled soda or sparkling water over the mixture.
  7. Stir gently to combine all the ingredients.
  8. Taste and adjust sugar or lime if needed.
  9. Garnish with a mint sprig and a slice of lime.
  10. Serve immediately while cold and refreshing.
